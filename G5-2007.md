Use hidpp10 protocol


# Buttons

9 buttons. None programmable but DPI buttons and Wheel tilt can be switched to generic buttons with 0x01 "Individual features".


# Resolution

 - 0x80: 400 dpi
 - 0x81: 800 dpi
 - 0x82: 1600 dpi
 - 0x83: 2000 dpi

Any other value gives ERR_INVALID_VALUE.
